October 24, 2025 Landscape

Yosemite National Park

My recent trip to Yosemite was absolutely breathtaking. The granite cliffs, waterfalls, and pristine wilderness exceeded all my expectations. Sadly didn't get to experience the night sky this time, but the daytime views were incredible.

Half Dome at sunrise - one of the most iconic views in Yosemite Valley

The first thing that struck me was the sheer scale of everything. The granite walls rise thousands of feet, making you feel incredibly small in the best possible way.

Early morning light on the valley

Golden hour in the valley

The scale of the granite walls is incredible

One last view before heading home

Even though I didn't get to see the night sky this time, the daytime views were absolutely worth the trip. Can't wait to go back!

October 20, 2025 Nature

Alviso Marina County Park

Alviso Marina County Park is such a hidden gem in the Bay Area. The wetlands and salt ponds create this incredible landscape that's perfect for photography, especially during golden hour.

The salt ponds create these amazing patterns and colors

The wildlife here is incredible - I saw so many different birds and the reflections in the water were perfect.

Wetland reflections

Salt pond patterns

Golden hour light on the wetlands

The peaceful beauty of the salt marshes

This place is perfect for anyone looking to escape the city and find some peace in nature. The light here is absolutely magical, especially during sunrise and sunset.

Agricultural patterns from above

Rolling hills and fields

Field textures and patterns

October 12, 2025 Local

Olive Tree Lane, Davis

Olive Tree Lane in Davis is one of my favorite spots for a walk, a run or a bike ride. The olive trees create these beautiful patterns and the light filtering through them is just magical.

The olive trees create these beautiful natural patterns

I love how the trees are arranged in these neat rows, creating these incredible leading lines and patterns.

Morning light through the olive trees

The symmetry of the olive grove

Golden hour light creates beautiful shadows

The endless rows create this incredible perspective

This place is perfect for practicing composition and working with natural patterns. The olive trees are so photogenic in any light!
